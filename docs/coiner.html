Universal Coin Acceptor

3D printable universal coin acceptor

Takes any coin, token or simply a washer and routes it right back into the return compartment or into the cash box, depending on the position of the coin guide.

Based on an original idea and concept by fluxgold
https://www.flippermarkt.de/community/forum/threads/muenzpruefer-einstellen-das-coin-durchfaellt-und-trotzdem-credit-gibt.224403/

Print notices:

  • Filament: PLA
  • Layer thickness: 0.2 mm; for better results at the bevels 0.1 or less
  • Supports: Below the retaining bar on the bottom plate and below the top plate's coin entrance bevel, depending on the printer
  • Bolt holes are optimized for both M3 and #4 screws
  • *.stl files are aligned in print position

The universal coin acceptor consists of three parts.

Back and front plates snap into each other.

Everything is hold together by screws through the microswitch.

The coin guide, placed on the left side, routes coins right back into the return box.

Built into an Arcade cabinet, with the microswitch wired directly to the  Jamma coin pin.

Even fits into a 60 years old EM pinball machine.

Coin switch

This device is designed to simply replace an already existing mechanical coin acceptor, with a coin switch already wired and mounted. If the purpose of the universal coin acceptor is to act as a piggy bank, letting any coin fall through into the cash box, there is no need to manipulate the switch.

If you want to have the coins routed back into the return compartment, unmount the original switch and screw it onto the universal coin acceptor. The wire needs to be bent into position.

To find a switch  somewhere near you, google for "coin acceptor microswitch".
