Quadro Jumbo

Quadro Jumbo auto monitor rotation


How to connect a  Quadro Jumbo to Pi2Jamma for auto monitor rotation with pinHP image.

Requirements: A 5 V DC single channel relay.

How it works: Quadro's monitor rotation is set by a simple hardware ON/OFF switch on the control board. As long as the switch is OFF, the monitor is kept in horizontal position or turned there, and vice versa. The switching is now taken over by a relay, controlled from pinHP image via GPIO-26.

Auto rotation demonstration  video

5 V single channel relay.

The relay is installed between Pi2Jamma and Quadro's control board.

The control board's monitor orientation switch is in OFF position.

Two wires are soldered to the back of the control board's monitor orientation switch, where two conductor tracks are connected. The third contact can be ignored - this is the original switch's OFF position, leading to nowhere.

Wires from the control board connect to COM (white) and NO (red). Connecting to COM and NC instead would default the monitor to vertical position and the High/Low level trigger required to be set to "L".

Wires from Pi2Jamma connect to 5V/DC+ (red), GND/DC- (black), IN/SIG (purple).

Pi2Jamma PCB: Wires from the relay, crimped to a Dupont plug, connect to 5V (red), GND (black), GPIO26 (purple).
